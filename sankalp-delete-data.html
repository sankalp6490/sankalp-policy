Sankalp — Account & Data Deletion

Sankalp lets you permanently delete your account and business data directly from within the app, without needing to contact support.

How to delete your data

  1. Open the Sankalp app and log in.
  2. Go to the Profile menu.
  3. Tap Delete Account (shown below the Logout option).
  4. Select a reason for leaving.
  5. Type DELETE MY ACCOUNT in the confirmation box.
  6. Tap Delete Account to confirm. This action is immediate and permanent.

What is retained, and why

For fraud prevention, abuse monitoring, and to comply with applicable legal and tax record-keeping obligations, we retain a minimal deletion record consisting of: your email address, business name, business category, city, and state, along with your stated reason for deleting the account. This limited record is kept securely and is not used for any purpose other than compliance and internal record-keeping.

No other personal or business data is retained after deletion. If you would like this minimal record removed as well, you may contact us using the details below and we will process the request in accordance with applicable law.
